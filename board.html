Operations

How We Run Projects

Team Structure

Each engagement is staffed with a dedicated project team of 3–5 members, led by a project manager who owns the client relationship.

Weekly Cadence

Teams meet internally and with clients weekly to maintain momentum, surface blockers early, and keep deliverables on track.

Quality Control

All final deliverables go through board-level review before client handoff to ensure output quality meets our standards.

Semester Cycles

We take on a new cohort of clients each semester (Fall & Spring), with intake happening in the first two weeks of each term.

Work With Us

Bring a challenge to our team.

Our board personally oversees every client engagement from scoping to delivery.
