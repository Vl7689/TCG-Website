Mission

What We Do

We exist to connect exceptional Georgia Tech students with organizations that need real analytical and technical horsepower — not just slide decks.

Why We Exist

Rigorous consulting work.
Student-driven. Results-focused.

The Technology Consulting Group at Georgia Tech bridges the gap between the talent in our university and the organizations that need it most. We deploy cross-functional student teams — combining strategy, data, and engineering — on semester-long engagements that produce real, usable outputs.

Our clients are founders validating markets, nonprofits optimizing fundraising, and hardware startups stress-testing designs. What they share: a high-value problem and an appetite for rigorous, honest analysis.

What makes TCG different

  • Semester-long engagements, not one-off workshops
  • Cross-functional teams across strategy, data, and engineering
  • Executive-ready deliverables from day one
  • Weekly check-ins to keep work on track
  • Georgia Tech's top talent across majors and disciplines

Mechanism design, CAD prototyping, actuation strategy, and technical tradeoff analysis. Our engineering sub-teams work directly on hardware and product development challenges.

Process design, stakeholder alignment, board communications, and top-of-funnel optimization. We help organizations run tighter operations and communicate more effectively.
